If left to reforest naturally at no cost, in 20 years the tract would yield 300 cubic metres per hectare of an inferior species worth only ¥20 per cubic metre.
If planted immediately at a cost of ¥1000 per hectare, in 20 years the tract would yield 400 cubic metres per hectare worth ¥25 per cubic metre.
If the discount rate is 5%(approximate value of the discount factor is 0.4 ), what is the benefit/cost ratio of the investment in planting?

<参考答案>
